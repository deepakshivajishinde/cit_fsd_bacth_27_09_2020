A reassessment of procedures at various stages of the election is being worked out by a team of officials in the Commission. The SVEEP (systematic voter education and electoral participation) strategy is being modified to ensure that ‘no voter is left behind’. The ECI guidelines pertaining to

electoral machinery, voters, political parties, candidates and other stakeholders are also being tweaked. We will step up the use of digital technologies in voter awareness, outreach and training. The Commission will make greater use of digital and media platforms, including bulk SMS, social media, television advertising, FM and community radio, NVSP (national voter service portal)
